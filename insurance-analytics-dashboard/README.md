## 📊 Key Metrics & KPIs

### Financial KPIs
| Metric | Definition | Business Impact |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Total Revenue** | Brokerage + Fees combined | Top-line performance |
| **Total Target** | Sum of all quotas | Goal alignment |
| **Achievement %** | (Revenue / Target) × 100 | Performance measure |
| **Invoice Sales** | Billed transactions | Cash flow indicator |

### Sales KPIs
| Metric | Definition | Business Impact |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **New Business** | Revenue from new policies | Growth indicator |
| **Cross-Sell** | Additional sales to existing customers | Revenue optimization |
| **Renewal** | Revenue from policy renewals | Customer retention |
| **Open Opportunities** | Active deals in pipeline | Future revenue |
| **Closed Opportunities** | Completed deals | Sales effectiveness |

### Activity KPIs
| Metric | Definition | Business Impact |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Meeting Count** | Total customer meetings | Engagement level |
| **Invoice Count** | Number of invoices | Transaction volume |
| **Meetings per Executive** | Activity by individual | Workload distribution |

### Pipeline KPIs
| Metric | Definition | Business Impact |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Win Ratio** | Closed / Total opportunities | Sales success rate |
| **Conversion Ratio** | Deals closed / Total in pipeline | Pipeline efficiency |
| **Stage Funnel** | Revenue by opportunity stage | Bottleneck identification |
